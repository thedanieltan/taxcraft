# Flat-rate PIT packages — wave 2

TaxCraft extends `@taxcraft/country-flat-rate` with six independently manifested packages for calendar years 2024 through 2026.

## Armenia

The Armenia package applies the general 20% income-tax rate to a caller-confirmed tax base. The official Tax Code has applied the 20% general rate since 1 January 2023.

Qualifying high-technology salary, dividends, rent, crypto-assets and other specially rated income remain outside coverage. The caller must resolve deductible income, social payments and funded-pension contributions before supplying the base.

## Georgia

The Georgia package applies the general 20% natural-person income-tax rate to caller-confirmed taxable income.

Residential rent, dividends, interest, small-business and micro-business regimes, pension contributions and other preferential or special treatments remain outside coverage.

## Moldova

The Moldova package applies the general 12% individual income-tax rate to caller-confirmed taxable income for 2024 through 2026.

The caller must derive taxable income after applicable exemptions and mandatory employee health-insurance deductions. Social-insurance calculations, independent-activity regimes, rent, royalties and other special or final-rate income remain outside coverage.

## North Macedonia

The North Macedonia package applies the general 10% personal-income-tax rate to a caller-confirmed category-specific tax base.

Gambling gains taxed at 15%, recognised-expense derivation, social contributions and payroll administration remain outside coverage.

## Ukraine

The Ukraine package applies the general 18% personal-income-tax rate to a caller-confirmed tax base.

The result deliberately excludes the military levy. It also excludes dividends, property transactions and other income subject to 0%, 5% or 9% rates, simplified entrepreneur regimes, deductions and tax social benefits.

## Uzbekistan

The Uzbekistan package applies the general 12% rate to caller-confirmed resident employment taxable income.

Non-resident and highly qualified foreign-worker rates, dividends, interest, material benefits, exemptions and employer withholding administration remain outside coverage.

## Shared boundaries

Each result is personal income tax only for the confirmed general-rate scope. TaxCraft does not determine residence, source, income classification, deduction eligibility, social contributions, filing obligations or foreign-tax credits. Inputs contain tax facts only and exclude names, identity numbers, addresses and tax documents.
