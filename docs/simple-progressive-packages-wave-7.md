# Simple-progressive PIT packages — wave 7

## Thailand ordinary personal income tax

The Thailand package calculates the ordinary progressive personal income tax for calendar years 2024, 2025 and 2026 from caller-confirmed annual taxable income.

## Input contract

The caller supplies:

- confirmation that the ordinary progressive schedule applies;
- annual taxable income after applicable deductions and allowances;
- confirmation, through the package scope, that the separate minimum-tax comparison is not required.

No names, identity numbers, residence records or tax documents are requested.

## Progressive schedule

- exempt through THB 150,000;
- 5% through THB 300,000;
- 10% through THB 500,000;
- 15% through THB 750,000;
- 20% through THB 1,000,000;
- 25% through THB 2,000,000;
- 30% through THB 4,000,000;
- 35% above THB 4,000,000.

## Exclusions

The package does not calculate or decide:

- taxable-income, deduction or allowance derivation;
- the separate 0.5% minimum-tax comparison for specified income categories;
- separately taxed or excluded income schedules;
- withholding credits, half-year payments, prior payments or refunds;
- residence, source, income classification or filing obligations.

## Sources

The maintained model uses Thailand Revenue Department rate guidance, its personal income tax guide and the official 2026 personal income tax forms index.
