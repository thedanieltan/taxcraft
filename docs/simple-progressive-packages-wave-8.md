# Simple-progressive packages — wave 8

## Fiji

TaxCraft implements Fiji individual income tax for calendar years 2024, 2025 and 2026 using the consolidated Pay As You Earn structure effective from 1 January 2024.

## Inputs

The package accepts:

- confirmation that the ordinary individual income-tax scope applies;
- a resident or non-resident schedule selector;
- annual chargeable income after applicable deductions.

The schedule selector is a calculation fact. TaxCraft does not request names, identity numbers, addresses or residency evidence.

## Resident schedule

The calculator applies:

- 0% through FJ$30,000;
- 18% from FJ$30,000 to FJ$50,000;
- 20% from FJ$50,000 to FJ$270,000;
- 33% from FJ$270,000 to FJ$300,000;
- 34% from FJ$300,000 to FJ$350,000;
- 35% from FJ$350,000 to FJ$400,000;
- 36% from FJ$400,000 to FJ$450,000;
- 37% from FJ$450,000 to FJ$500,000;
- 38% from FJ$500,000 to FJ$1 million;
- 39% above FJ$1 million.

## Non-resident schedule

The non-resident schedule applies 20% from the first dollar through FJ$270,000, followed by the same 33% through 39% high-income bands.

## Exclusions

The package does not determine:

- chargeable income or deductions;
- residence, source or temporary-resident status;
- PAYE withholding or annual reconciliation;
- prior payments, refunds or filing balances;
- foreign-tax credits or treaty relief;
- business-return and category-specific income treatment.

## Sources

The package is grounded in Fiji Revenue and Customs Service material covering the new PAYE structure effective from 1 January 2024, current personal-income-tax guidance and the current income-tax legislation library.

Implementation, deployment and live acceptance remain separate states.
